Sweet Lover's Guide: Must-Try Desserts in NYC

by Phoebe Yaheng Wu

If you're new to New York or have been here for a while, this blog can help you discover some of the best desserts in the city. Without further ado, let's dive into the sweet world of New York City's best desserts!

1. Harbs

Harbs is a Japanese pastry shop with several locations in NYC, and they are known for their Mille Crepes. Harbs' version of the Mille Crepes is filled with fresh fruits and cream, which gives it a light and refreshing taste that is not too sweet. If you're looking for a unique and delicious dessert experience in NYC, Harbs is definitely worth checking out!

Website: https://www.harbsnyc.com/
Address: 465 W Broadway, New York, NY 10012
Price: ~$15/cake

2. Van Leeuwen

When it comes to ice cream, nothing beats Van Leeuwen's honeycomb flavor. It's hands down my all-time favorite. Trust me, if you're looking for a scoop of pure indulgence, this is it. Your taste buds will thank you for it!

Website: https://vanleeuwenicecream.com/
Address: 1250 6th Ave, New York, NY 10020
Price: ~$6/scoop

3. Magnolia Bakery

Magnolia's banana pudding is one of the most iconic desserts you can find in New York City. It's a treat that people will line up for hours just to savor its creamy goodness. If you're a fan of sweet treats, then you simply can't miss out on trying this legendary dessert.

Website: https://www.magnoliabakery.com/
Address: 1240 6th Ave, New York, NY 10020
Price: ~$6/small cup

4. Beard Papa's

If you're looking for some of the best cream puffs in town, look no further than Beard Papa's. As a popular Japanese bakery chain with locations around the world, including several in New York City, their cream puffs are known and loved by pastry enthusiasts worldwide. As a seasoned cream puff lover myself, I can personally vouch for the deliciousness of Beard Papa's and highly recommend a visit to this bakery.

Website: https://www.beardpapas.com/
Address: 11 St Marks Pl, New York, NY 10003
Price: ~$5/cream puff
